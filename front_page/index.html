How PhantomClick Works ?

Stage 1 : Scam-Brain (SMS Analysis)

Upload the suspicious SMS screenshot. PhantomClick extracts text, detects URLs or phone numbers, identifies the scam category, and flags psychological urgency using AI-powered analysis.

Stage 2 : URL Scorecard (Risk Analysis)

The detected link is analyzed without opening it. PhantomClick checks domain age, look-alike patterns, SSL validity, and URL structure to generate a clear risk score (0‚Äì100).

Stage 3 : Ghost-View (Safe Replay)

High-risk links are opened inside a secure sandbox. PhantomClick visually highlights dangerous fields and generates a step-by-step replay showing how the scam would unfold.

Stage 4 : Digital Witness (Forensic Report)

All findings are compiled into a professional forensic PDF with screenshots, risk scores, and an AI-written summary‚Äîready to be submitted as evidence on cybercrime.gov.in.
